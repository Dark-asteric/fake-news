Lionel Messi leaves Inter Miami and announces his surprise transfer to Real Madrid !!!

Sofia Turner

11 Decembre 2025 – 08h42 (GMT: +1)

In a completely unexpected turn of events, Lionel Messi announced this morning his departure from Inter Miami to join Real Madrid, a choice that has shaken both MLS and the Spanish league. The news, confirmed by the Madrid club at daybreak, has sent shockwaves through the international football community.

Since arriving in the United States in 2023, Messi had helped explode the popularity of MLS. His sudden departure marks the end of a major chapter for Inter Miami, which loses its iconic figure.

At a press conference held in Madrid, Messi spoke briefly to justify his decision:"After two intense seasons in Miami, I felt a new challenge was calling me. Real Madrid presented me with an ambitious project, and I decided to seize this opportunity."

According to sources close to the negotiations, the contract is for two years, with an option for an additional season. No financial details have been disclosed, but several Spanish media outlets are describing the deal as "exceptionally expensive."

Real Madrid is embracing a historic choice. Real Madrid's sporting director, Javier Ramos Ortega (fictional character), spoke shortly after the official announcement. "This signing is much more than symbolic. Lionel Messi remains a player capable of influencing any match. We are convinced that he will bring his experience and leadership to a team undergoing a period of renewal," said Ramos Ortega.

He also stressed that the discussions had been conducted "in the utmost discretion" for several months.


Analyse – BBC Sport

Messi's transfer from Inter Miami to Real Madrid is one of the most surprising moves in recent years. While many thought the Argentinian would finish his career in MLS, his return to Europe – and even more so to his historic rival FC Barcelona – gives this decision an almost mythical dimension.



Real Madrid hopes to bolster its attacking prowess after a disappointing Champions League season, while Inter Miami now has to rethink its entire sporting project. Reactions in the United States and Spain: In Miami, fans say they are "stunned" by the news, with some believing the club should have done everything possible to keep its captain.

Local media are already talking about a potential drop in stadium attendance. In Madrid, however, the atmosphere is quite different: a crowd is expected tonight at the Santiago Bernabéu for the official presentation of the player.
